Sound Engineer

Familiar with both analogue and most digital consoles, mixing live, corporate and theatre are all regular activities. Most experienced with Yamaha consoles (CL, QL, M7, PM5 and LS9) but versed in Midas, Soundcraft and SSL. I am just as happy running monitors as FoH, and can mix any genre band, working to each artists unique style.

As an RF Technician looking after Radio Mics and In Ear Monitors (IEMs) I am familiar with Sennheiser and Shure transmitters and receivers, and with DPA microphones. As part of this I conform to site and temporary RF licences to maximise performance, minimise interferance and stay within the law.

System Tech

Experienced in designing, setting up, and tuning systems in anything from a small pub, through to 3000 person halls. I am most familiar with d&b Audiotechnik PA having designed, installed and tuned numerous Y, Q, J and E series systems over a 5 year period. Completely fluent in use of ArrayCalc, NoiseCalc and R1 Remote software to make maximum use of all features. If you require PA to be provided for your event I have contacts with various hire companies around the UK and can help arrange equipment to meet you or your client's needs.

Lighting Designer

First and foremost a theatre lighting designer, with occasional lighting design for live, colour and form are prevalent themes in any Will Lay design.

Capable of independently operating and programming any of the ETC Eos family consoles (Element, Ion, Gio, Eos Ti) designs for theatrical or corporate shows can be designed, rigged, programmed and operated.
