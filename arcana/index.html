About Will

Will was born and raised north of London in Hertforshire, UK, but now lives in Bath, UK, while studing for his Electronic & Electrical Engineering MEng degree at the University of Bath.

This website acts as a place for collecting together projects and work, and write a little about Will's work, hobbies, and any thing else deemed relevant!

Will's biggest hobby is supporting live and theatre events with Backstage Technical Services (BTS) in Bath, and with Hertford Dramatic & Operatic Society (HD&OS) in Hertford. This hobby began aged 14 as Assistant Lighting Designer for a youth theatre production of "The Lion, The Witch, and The Wardrobe", which set the scene for the folowing years.

On the rare ocasion when Will is not studying, working behind the scenes, or is behind a camera, he also works as a Telethon Caller for the University of Bath Alumni Fund, helping to update Alumni on upcoming events and the progress of the University.
