Production Technician

Production Technician

William is an experienced live event and theatre production Tehnician based in Bath, UK, particularly specialised in sound, lighting and show technincal management.

Primarily a Sound Engineer, Will is experienced in designing, setting up, and tuning systems in anything from a small pub, through to 3000 person halls. Familiar with both analogue and most digital consoles, mixing live and theatre are both regular activities.

I am most familiar with D&B Audiotechnik PA, having designed, installed and tuned numerous Y, Q, and E series systems over a 5 year period. Completely fluent in use of ArrayCalc, NoiseCalc and R1 Remote software to make maximum use of all features. If you require PA to be provided for your event I have contacts with various hire companies around the UK and can help arrange equipment to meet you or your client's needs.

Although primarily a Sound Engineer, William is also capable in the following areas:

  • Lighting Design for theatre and live event.
  • Operating Avolites Pearl 2004/2008 and Avolites Titan consoles for Live.
  • Programming and operating ETC Eos Consoles for Theatre.
  • Power system design for live events.
  • Video infrastrucutre and output for Live or Theatre.

Qualifications

William holds the following current qualifications:

  • IPAF: 3a, 3b, PAV
  • Fire Awareness
  • PAT - Stage Electrics "for the Events Industry" course

References or example work is available on request.
