Photographer

William is an experienced photographer, who specialises in unobtrusive event photography. Other work includes headshots, landscapes, and sport.

Experienced Event Photographer, working to capture memories - whether it is at a party, awards ceremony, or at a theatre show William is able to capture those moments so they can last you a lifetime.

Previous clients have included:

  • Red Bull
  • University of Bath Students' Union
  • Swindon Comic Con
  • IamVIP
  • Hertford Dramatic & Operatic Society

Large or small, any project is special and can be catered for with photos which will capture moments perfectly.
